#Exterior Decoration

Exterior decoration means determine the look and feel of a home.Exterior decorators help determine the look and feel of a client's home by selecting and placing decorative elements such as paint, textiles, and furniture. This client-facing role involves meeting directly with clients to get a sense of how they intend to use their space and how they want it to feel. These exterior design elements include light, colour, texture and pattern,shape,form; and keeping them balanced is the key to creating an aesthetically pleasing exterior. Exterior decoration is the painting of the outside of a building.Exterior design will include the design of your garden, deck and patio. One of the elements of exterior decoration is the right fabric for outdoor furniture.

Adding lots of different textures helps to make a look more expensive and feel, cozier.The space feel more like home and add an extra layer of style. The principles of design are simple guidelines used to organize the various elements of design (light,texture, color, etc.) in a composition.

6 Best Exterior Design Ideas & Trends

1. Outdoor Seating Bench

When the house and guests enter the front door, they are welcomed. Highlights and adds drama to interesting landscape or architectural features. Helps to create a comfortable and communal setting, such as a defined seat or recreation area.

3. Stairs Lighting

Placing LED strips at the base of your stairs or under furniture is an easy way to brighten up your backyard or garden and make the space more secure. It creates a super modern look and transforms the field area into a place that can be enjoyed all day and night.

4. Lantern

It is better to use the lantern as a source of outside light because it is easy to move, they set the mood and it came in a wide style. Depending on the look you are going for and how much light you want from them you can find lanterns that use either LED lights or candles.

5. Orbs & Spheres

Create an ethereal look in your backyard, garden or your yard using rounds and orbs. You can hang them separately or cluster them in groups on the ground to set up mysterious vibes and create a perfect small oasis.

6. Pots And Plants

You are likely to plant a garden in your yard. Why not let them have a dual duty as both a planter and a light source. They come in large sizes so that no matter if you are planting a few flowers or a large number of hedges, a planter is ready for the job.
