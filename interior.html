#Interior Decoration

Interior decoration means determine the look and feel of a home.Interior decorators help determine the look and feel of a client's home by selecting and placing decorative elements such as paint, textiles, and furniture. This client-facing role involves meeting directly with clients to get a sense of how they intend to use their space and how they want it to feel. These interior design elements include light, colour, texture and pattern,shape,form; and keeping them balanced is the key to creating an aesthetically pleasing interior. Adding lots of different textures helps to make a room look more expensive and feel, cozier, so don't be afraid to throw in lots of decorative pillows, chunky knit throws, blankets, and thick, luxurious drapes â€“ it will make the space feel more like home and add an extra layer of style. The principles of design are simple guidelines used to organize the various elements of design (light,texture, color, etc.) in a composition.

Use mirrors to balance a fixed color scheme while adding a polished look to any modern living room. Instead of adding too much art, stick with one large piece that becomes the main focal point. Add an eclectic mix of pillows to draw attention to a couch that is naturally simple in design.

#2. Elements of Interior design

Color

Color is often deemed to be an important element of design.When choosing wall and flooring colors, it's important to keep the goal of the space in mind.Warm tones, like red, orange and yellow can energize a space and its occupants. Cool tones generally create quiet, relaxing atmospheres.Cool color such as blue, green and purple.

Shape

Shapes plays a basic role in planning interiors. Most buildings and rooms are shaped as rectangles, the width and height decide the accessories and their dimensions. That will help them fit in effortlessly in the particular space.hapes can successfully create an outstanding and effective graphic design. The mood and message of the design may be enhanced by altering its shape in terms of form, colour and size. Shapes may be curved, rounded, angled or sharp, and each conveys a different type of mood or emotion

Space

Space planning is one of the most important in interior design. It can have a very strong impact on the success of a room.Knowledge of furniture sizes and space allowances, some imagination and a sense of composition really help too.Space is one of the most important elements of interior design. Space acts as a base on which the entire interior design plan is built.

Pattern

In commercial interior design, pattern is often applied using wallcoverings, tile, carpeting, and other graphic elements.Patterns add drama, create depth and are an effective way to unify an interior scheme. ... Pattern gives you the ability to bring energy, interest and contrast into your design, and when patterns are done well they bring a room together and give depth to your space.

Form

The form is nothing but a shape that is, in fact, the very basis of any interior design. It provides a basic outline of the three-dimensional thing or object in space. You can achieve it by combining one or more shapes which are further enhanced by adding other elements such as patterns, colors, and textures.An interior designer must know how to utilize these lines to define the forms.
